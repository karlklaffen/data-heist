Deep learning unsuccessful for this dataset.

  → RF outperforms NN by 1402.67 R² points

Errors (NN vs RF):
  Latitude:  0.239 vs 0.016 mean error (15x worse)
  Longitude: 0.606 vs 0.012 mean error (49x worse!)
  Euclidean: 0.651 vs 0.020 (32x worse)

Why NN Failed:
  - Weak signal (0.149 max correlation) insufficient for deep learning
  - Small dataset (8000 samples) insufficient for neural net regularization
  - Gradient-based learning struggles with weak gradients from weak signal
  - Tree models naturally handle feature interactions better than NN

CONCLUSION: Random Forest is definitively the best model for this data.
Tree-based methods superior to deep learning for weak-signal problems.
